Filters

Filters change what will get printed to the screen. They are denoted by a pipe, then the name of the filter, and then arguments if there are any.

| title



{{ "the lord of the rings" | title }}

The Lord Of The Rings

| capitalize



{{ "frodo" | capitalize }}

{% set sam = "samwise" %}
{{ sam | capitalize }}

Frodo
Samwise

| default



{% set ringwraith = null %}
{{ ringwraith | default('The Witch King') }}


{% set sauron = "The Dark Lord" %}
{{ sauron | default("This won't display.") }}

The Witch King
The Dark Lord

| lower & | upper



{{ "THIS WILL BE LOWERCASE" | lower }}


{{ "this will be uppercase" | upper }}

this will be lowercase
THIS WILL BE UPPERCASE

| striptags



{{ "<p>Roads go ever <u>on</u> and <em>on</em> (<u>with</u> formatting).</p>" }}


{{ "<p>Roads go ever <u>on</u> and <em>on</em> (<u>without</u> formatting).</p>" | striptags }}

Roads go ever on and on (with formatting).

Roads go ever on and on (without formatting).

| nl2br (new line to <br>)

Sample code


{{
   "Roads go ever ever on,
    Over rock and under tree,
    By caves where never sun has shone,
    By streams that never find the sea;
    Over snow by winter sown,
    And through the merry flowers of June,
    Over grass and over stone,
    And under mountains in the moon." | nl2br
}}

Without nl2br

Roads go ever ever on, Over rock and under tree, By caves where never sun has shone, By streams that never find the sea; Over snow by winter sown, And through the merry flowers of June, Over grass and over stone, And under mountains in the moon.

With nl2br

Roads go ever ever on,
Over rock and under tree,
By caves where never sun has shone,
By streams that never find the sea;
Over snow by winter sown,
And through the merry flowers of June,
Over grass and over stone,
And under mountains in the moon.
