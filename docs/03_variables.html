Variables are like addresses where you can find (and put) bits of information (data). They can store something as simple as, "hello" or 42, or very complicated arrays or objects.

  • Arrays ([ ]) - Think of them as lists of values
  • Objects ({ }) - A group of key, value pairs ({key: 'value', nextKey, 'nextValue'}).

The values of arrays can be strings, numbers or other arrays or objects.
