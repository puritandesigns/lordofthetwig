The Lord of the Twig

You can use ~ to combine strings between {{ }}. 


{% set Hobbits = {
    frodo: 'Frodo',
    samwise: 'Sam',
    merry: 'Merry',
    pippin: 'Pippin'
} %}

The four Hobbits who travelled out of the Shire are
{{ Hobbits.frodo ~ ", " ~ Hobbits.samwise ~ ", " ~ Hobbits.merry ~ ", and " ~ Hobbits.pippin ~ "." }}

{#
Or you could write it like this:

The four Hobbits who travelled out of the Shire are
{{ Hobbits.frodo }}, {{ Hobbits.samwise }}, {{ Hobbits.merry }}, and {{ Hobbits.pippin }}.
#}
The four Hobbits who travelled out of the Shire are Frodo, Sam, Merry, and Pippin.
